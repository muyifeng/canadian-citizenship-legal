Privacy Policy

Canada Citizenship 2026 (the “App”) · Last updated: July 4, 2026

The short version. The App has no accounts and no login. It shows no ads and uses no third-party analytics or advertising trackers. Your study progress, test date, and settings are stored only on your device. The only information that leaves your device is what is needed to process an optional in-app purchase, handled by Apple/Google and our purchase provider RevenueCat.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how. It applies to the Canada Citizenship 2026 mobile app for iOS and Android. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Who we are

The App is an independent study aid published by the developer identified in the App Store and Google Play listings. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Government of Canada or Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC). You can reach us using the contact details at the bottom of this page.

2. Information we collect

Information stored on your device

The following is created as you use the App and is kept locally on your device. We do not have access to it and it is not sent to us:

  • Your study progress, quiz history, and scores
  • Your chosen test date, language, and theme preferences
  • App settings and onboarding state

If you use the App’s “Export my data” feature, this information is written to a file that you choose to save or share through your device’s share sheet. Where it goes from there is under your control.

Purchase information

If you buy a subscription or the lifetime upgrade, the purchase is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see your card number or full payment details. To unlock and restore purchases, we use RevenueCat, which receives a randomly generated (anonymous) app user ID and the purchase/receipt information needed to manage your entitlement, along with basic device and country information provided by the app store.

Feedback you choose to send

If you submit feedback about a question or lesson, the note you write and the App version may be used to help us correct and improve the study content. Please do not include sensitive personal information in feedback.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, address, or account — the App has no sign-up
  • No advertising identifiers and no ad networks
  • No third-party analytics, behavioural tracking, or cross-app tracking
  • No precise location

3. How your information is used

  • To run the App and save your progress on your device
  • To unlock, verify, and restore in-app purchases
  • To fix and improve study content when you send feedback

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it for advertising.

4. Text-to-speech

The App can read lessons and questions aloud using your device’s built-in text-to-speech engine (provided by Apple or Google). This runs through the operating system; the App does not send that text to any server of ours.

5. AI-generated content

Some study highlights and key points in the App are generated with the help of AI in advance and shipped with the App as fixed content. This happens during content preparation, before release. The App does not send your study data, feedback, or any other information to AI services while you use it, and we do not process your data with AI at any time.

6. Third-party services

The limited data described above is handled by these providers under their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store — apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play — policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data retention and deletion

  • On-device data: use “Clear local data” in the App, or delete the App, to remove everything stored on your device.
  • Purchase records: retained by RevenueCat and the app stores for as long as needed to manage your entitlement and meet legal/accounting obligations.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for people preparing for the Canadian citizenship test and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International users

Our purchase provider and the app stores may process purchase data on servers outside your country, including the United States. By making a purchase you consent to that processing.

10. Your rights

Because your study data lives on your device, you can access it in the App, export it at any time, and delete it with “Clear local data” or by uninstalling. For questions about purchase data held by RevenueCat, contact us and we will help.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
