Data Deletion Request

Canada Citizenship 2026 (the “App”), published by ByteLabs · Last updated: July 19, 2026

How to request deletion. The App has no accounts and no login. Almost all of your data lives only on your device and you can delete it yourself in seconds. For the small amount of purchase data handled off your device, email bytelabsapp@gmail.com and we will delete it.

This page explains, for Canada Citizenship 2026 by ByteLabs, how to request deletion of your data and what is deleted or kept.

Steps to request deletion

  1. On-device data (study progress, test date, settings). In the App, open Profile and tap Clear local data, or simply uninstall the App. Either action permanently deletes all of this data from your device immediately. This data never leaves your device, so nothing further is needed.
  2. Purchase data (if you bought a subscription or the Lifetime unlock). Email bytelabsapp@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my data” and tell us the platform (iOS or Android). We will delete the purchase records our provider holds for you and confirm by reply.

What is deleted, and what is kept

DataDeletedRetention
Study progress, test date, app settings (on-device) Immediately, on “Clear local data” or uninstall None — stored only on your device
Purchase history + an anonymous purchase identifier (held by our purchase provider, RevenueCat) On email request Kept only while needed to provide your purchase/entitlement and to meet legal and accounting obligations; deleted from RevenueCat on request
App Store / Google Play transaction records Handled by Apple / Google Retained by the app stores under their own policies and applicable tax/financial-records law; outside our control

The App collects no name, email, contacts, location, photos, or other personal profile — so there is no account or profile to delete. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Contact

Requests or questions: bytelabsapp@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy and Support.
